ADVANTAGE OF WPC

GreenTech has 2 series of WPC, we call it capped WPC (new technology) and un-capped WPC (Conventional WPC).

·GreenTech Capped WPC is light-years ahead of earlier, uncapped one. WPC or wood plastic composite is an eco-friendly alternative to wood decking.

·Whether capped or uncapped, GreenTech WPC is made primarily from wood flour and recycled plastic polymers.

·The big difference is that capped products have a "cover" that provides ultra- protection against the elements and everyday living.

·It's an important distinction to note, because earlier generations of composite decking were uncapped.

·As a result, they were more vulnerable to staining and fading.

·Of course, when we say cap or "cover" we don’t mean something that is added after the composite decking is made, the cap is bonded to the core during the manufacturing process.

GreenTech Capped WPC

Co-extrusion process creates strong, resilient boards with lasting good looks.

·So, how are Greentech capped composite deck boards made? At GREENTECH’s factory, the wood and polyethylene polymers are carefully blended to create a solid, dense core material.

·The core material is then co-extruded with a patent-pending, non-organic surface product.

·This is another important distinction to note.

·GreenTech contains UV inhibitors, anti-oxidants, and a careful blend of color added at precisely the right time – a detail that gives boards their rich color and dramatic, multi-chromatic streaking.
